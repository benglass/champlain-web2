Assignment 1: Content Generation

Students will create content for top level pages from the Site Map we generated collaboratively during class. Each student will be responsible for generating content for two pages. In addition, students will be responsible for generating some additional content elements such as quotes and student work.

The content for each page must include the following elements:

  • At least 3 paragraphs (3-4 sentences each)
  • At least 1 image
  • At least 1 in text link. Links can be indicated as follows: "Interested in checking out the campus? Take a look at the 'Visit Us' page for more information [link to 'Visit']"
  • 1 top level page heading (the title of the page)
  • At least 1 second level heading

All students will complete the following pieces of content

  • One 2-3 Sentence quote about their experience in the program
  • Two pieces of student work for use in the Student Work section, submitted as high res JPG files

Additionall, individual students are responsible for generating content for the following pages

  • Jon Testa: Program, Capstone
  • Jolee Main: Facilities, Studio
  • Morgan Foote: Faculty, Home
  • Michael LeGrand: Apply, Visit
  • Teresa Coathup: Student Work, Alumn Profiles

Content should be generated as a Microsoft Word (doc, docx), Rich Text Format (rtf) or Google Doc and submited via email to the instructor.
